Website Planning Document

Site Name

Lagos Garden Guide

I chose this name because it clearly communicates the geographical focus (Lagos) and the primary purpose (gardening guidance). The alliteration makes it memorable and easy to pronounce, while directly conveying to visitors that this is a resource specifically tailored for gardening in Lagos, Nigeria.

Optional domain availability: lagosgardeguide.com

Site Purpose

Lagos Garden Guide serves as a comprehensive resource for beginner and intermediate gardeners in Lagos, Nigeria. The site provides location-specific information about growing vegetables and maintaining indoor plants in Lagos' unique climate. Users will find practical guides on getting started with gardening, vegetable cultivation techniques suitable for Lagos' weather patterns, indoor plant care specific to local conditions, and solutions to region-specific gardening challenges. The site aims to empower Lagos residents to grow their own food and beautify their living spaces through accessible, practical gardening knowledge.

Scenarios

  • I live in an apartment in Lagos with a small balcony. What vegetables can I grow in containers that will thrive in this environment?
  • What indoor plants are best suited for Lagos' humidity and can survive with inconsistent electricity for air conditioning?
  • How do I adjust my gardening schedule and practices between Lagos' intense rainy season and dry season?
  • Where can I source quality gardening supplies and seeds in Lagos at reasonable prices?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E7D32 (Forest Green)

Secondary Color: #FFA000 (Amber)

Accent Color: #6A1B9A (Purple)

Background Color: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)

Color Usage

  • Primary Color (#2E7D32): Headers, navigation elements, and primary buttons - represents plant life and growth
  • Secondary Color (#FFA000): Subheadings, call-to-action elements, and highlights - represents the Lagos sunshine
  • Accent Color (#6A1B9A): Special features, links, and decorative elements - represents flower blossoms
  • Background Color (#F5F5F5): Page background and content areas - provides a clean, neutral backdrop
