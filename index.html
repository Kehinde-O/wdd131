Lagos, Nigeria

Nigeria, the "Giant of Africa," boasts a rich heritage with ancient civilizations like the Nok culture and powerful kingdoms such as the Oyo Empire. Colonized by the British in the 19th century, Nigeria gained independence on October 1, 1960, and is now Africa's largest economy. Lagos, the economic hub, started as the Yoruba settlement "Eko." Named "Lagos" by Portuguese explorers in the 15th century, it became a British colony in 1861. Today, Lagos is known for its vibrant culture and bustling markets.
